About Us

Welcome to GuidedSoft, your dedicated partner in achieving IT excellence.

GuidedSoft is more than just a coaching and consulting firm; it's a community of IT professionals, aspiring talents, and experts who are committed to supporting one another on the path to IT career transformation. Our mission is to empower individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to thrive in the ever-evolving IT job market.

Our coaching and consulting services are designed to meet the unique needs of each individual. We offer expert guidance, hands-on training, career assessments, interview preparation, job placement assistance, and ongoing support. We don't just focus on teaching technical skills; we emphasize the importance of understanding the industry, mastering relevant tools, and nurturing essential soft skills.

Whether you are an experienced IT professional looking to take the next step in your career or someone who aspires to enter the IT field, GuidedSoft is your go-to resource.

Our team is passionate about your success, and we're dedicated to providing the tools and knowledge you need to excel in the ever-competitive IT job market. Join us at GuidedSoft and take a transformative journey toward IT excellence. We're excited to have you on board, and we're here to support you every step of the way.

IT Career Coaching

Our IT Career Coaching service is designed for individuals seeking personalized guidance to kickstart or advance their IT careers. We provide one-on-one mentoring, skill assessments, and tailored action plans to help you reach your professional goals in the IT industry.

Technical Training and Workshops

Our Technical Training and Workshops cover a broad spectrum of IT domains, from DevOps and cloud computing to software development and quality assurance. With hands-on training and interactive sessions, you'll gain practical skills essential for success in the IT job market.

Resume Building and Interview Preparation

Landing your dream IT job starts with an impressive resume and solid interview skills. We offer guidance in crafting impactful resumes that showcase your qualifications. Our Interview Preparation service equips you with the knowledge and confidence needed to ace technical interviews.

Job Placement and Support

Our Job Placement and Support service is designed to connect you with promising IT job opportunities. We provide resources and support to help you secure interviews, negotiate offers, and succeed in your new role. We're with you every step of the way in your journey to IT employment.

DevOps Coaching for Seamless Software Delivery

Unlock the full potential of DevOps with our dedicated coaching services. We'll guide you through the intricacies of DevOps practices, from continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD) to automation and infrastructure as code (IaC). Our hands-on, personalized approach will empower you to streamline software delivery pipelines, reduce lead times, and enhance the quality of your digital products.

Whether you're a beginner or looking to level up your DevOps skills, our coaching ensures you master the essential tools and principles for success in the world of DevOps. Start your journey with us today and accelerate your career in the IT industry.

Consulting and Placement Services: Your Bridge to IT Excellence

At GuidedSoft, we provide expert consulting and placement services designed to be your guiding light in the complex IT landscape. Whether you're an organization seeking top-notch IT talent or an IT professional looking for your next career move, our consulting and placement services have got you covered. We work closely with businesses to understand their unique IT requirements and connect them with the most suitable candidates.

Our IT professionals undergo rigorous training and mentoring to ensure they're equipped with the latest industry trends and best practices.

GuidedSoft is where talent meets opportunity, ensuring the perfect match between employers and candidates in the ever-evolving world of IT.
